Plantain

Maintain your science plans easily and efficiently.

Plantain builds science plans by combining national templates with user specific content.

Plantain uses approved USGS national data plans as templates. Users can add specific content that gets merged with the national plan of interest. This allows users to focus more time and energy writing their specific content additions instead of on the plan in its entirety.

Plantain uses various national data plans, such as the USGS Quality Assurance Plan and the Data Mangagement Plan. Users can also view other users plans for reference and comparison.

View National Plans

Plantain has various templated national plans that can be viewed including the USGS quality assurance plan and data managment plan.

Add Specific Content

Users fill out forms to add specific content to a national template plan of interest.

Merge specific content with national

Plantain merges user specific content into the appropriate location within the national plan.
